Website Evaluations

Yale School of Art Website

Yale School of Art's webite is used to give out information to those looking to attend Yale's School of Art graduate school course. It contains the work of some of the previous students who went to Yale School of Art. There are many visuals and bright vibrant colors.

  • C- Contrast

    • It is very hard to read the content of the pages because of there is not enough contrast witht he baackground and the text. It is hard to focus on ehat you are trying to read because some words blend in witht the surroundings.

  • R- Repition

    • There is no real repition except for the style of the boxes. While that is a repeating factor, it is not a good one as it messes with the visuals of the website.

  • A- Alignment

    • The alignment is not good on this website. Everything is set on the left side of the screen on every page, where you can scroll to the next bit of random information they have.

  • P- Proximity

    • While everything is grouped together in text boxes, there is no real order to what the inforamtion is set in. Then, when you click on the pages in the navigation, everything is sort of forced together.


Soccer.com

Soccer.com is a website where soccer players can go and buy gear to help improve training, improve performance, or even support their team. There is a possible membership that you can sign up for that can set you up to get discounts on future purposes. This site has many different pages, and is locatd at the top of the page.

  • C- Contrast

    • The contrast on Soccer.com is strong, making every word vissible. The readability is easy on the eye, as the size of the words is good with contrasting dark and light colors for the background and words.

  • R- Repition

    • The repition on this website is consistent and appealing. The combination of black, white, and red is easy and iconic for the brand.

  • A- Alignment

    • The alignment is even and efficient to include the maximum amount of information in one section to where it is not overwhelming. The categories are organized and lined up perfectly.

  • P- Proximity

    • The proximity on Soccer.com is as good as it gets. There are multiple categories that have sub categories that can help you get to what you need as quick as possible.
